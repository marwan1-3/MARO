What is a Smart Home?

A smart home is a residence that uses smart devices and technology to enable remote management and monitoring of systems and appliances. Homeowners can control lighting, heating, security, and more from their smartphones or computers.

Benefits of a Smart Home

  • Remote Control: Manage your devices from anywhere.
  • Security: Smart security systems provide continuous monitoring.
  • Energy Efficiency: Helps reduce energy consumption.
